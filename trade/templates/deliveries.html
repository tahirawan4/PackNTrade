DELIVERIES

All orders will be delivered within 1 – 2 working days Monday to Friday only. Same day dispatch is only for orders placed before 11am. Orders are delivered via Parcel Force or DPD weekdays (Monday to Friday only). No orders will be delivered on Weekends (Saturday & Sunday). Free delivery is only for UK Mainland orders over £150 + VAT. Any orders below £150 + VAT will be charged flat shipping rate of £9.99

If your order has to be on the pallet than the cost of pallet delivery will be £65 + VAT UK Mainland only. Free delivery is only available for orders within UK Mainland. It does not apply to Europe, Scottish Highlands and Islands, Northern Ireland, Southern Ireland, Isle of Man and all offshore Isles or Europe.

All online orders are processed using world pay merchant services and they might check and verify your payments for fraud protection. If your payment will not pass through the fraud protection system than your order will not be dispatched until it has passed. We will notify you of any delay in processing your order.

Outside UK Mainland Orders

European orders and orders outside UK Mainland can only be placed via email and not through the website. Delivery cost for EU Orders and orders outside UK Mainland will be calculated on the total weight of the shipment.

European customers with a VAT number who require 0% UK VAT rate will have to send us the following details in order to qualify for 0% VAT.

  • 1. Company Name
  • 2. Company Address
  • 3. Company Registration Number
  • 4. Company VAT Number

These details are mandatory for you to provide us to make sure that you are eligible 0% VAT rate.

PLEASE NOTE:

It is the customer’s responsibility to receive the delivery or to have someone arranged to receive the delivery. In any case we will not be liable for any extra costs that may occur for rearranging the delivery if someone was unable to receive it the first time.

Orders received by 11am will usually be dispatched the same day, provided that goods are in stock. We will notify you of any delay in processing your order if an item is out of stock. You will receive email notification when your order is received and you will also be sent notification when your goods are dispatched.

Out of Stock Items

We usually have all the items in stock but if in case after receiving the order we found out that the item you have ordered is out of stock than we will notify you immediately before processing your order. If you are planning to collect then we advise that you phone in advance to ensure items are in stock.
